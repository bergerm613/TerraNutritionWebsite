Who We Are

We are not just a company. We are a mission, personified. What is that mission?

"As long as I am on this Earth, I will care, I will love, I will do what I can to make it better."

When Shifra J. Orlian RD CDN named this company name in 1997, she set out to embody this passion at the forefront of whatever we do. Whatever your unique needs, the Terra team will take care of it with our unparalled and genuine care. Our aim is to make your journey better for you.

Shifra's corporate and personal experiences have enabled her to successfully help and respond to all of the varied needs of her individual clients, whatever they are: menus, meal plans, ensuring food safety, regulatory processes, and more. Whether a facility requesting assistance or menus, an individual in need of nutriotional guidance after a new diagnosis, or some basic nutritional help, our mission statement guides us.
